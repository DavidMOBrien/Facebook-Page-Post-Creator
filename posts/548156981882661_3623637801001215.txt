Tristen Perreault is a 5th semester Glee Clubber who, even during the pandemic, finds ways to share his love for music. Utilizing his skills as a musician, Tristen creates some fun "musical shenanigans," as he calls them, and publishes them to Instagram, Youtube, and Facebook. In his most recent upload, Tristen has done a cover of Dean Summerwind's "Parked Out By The Lake," and features fellow Glee Club members: Sam Trainer, Gabe Trainer, and Brett Biermann!
