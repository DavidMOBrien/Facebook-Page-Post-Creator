What do you like most about the Glee Club?
The thing I love most about the Glee Club is the guys' passion for the group. The brotherhood amongst the singers is unmatched. It's amazing to sing in a choir that values both camaraderie and musical excellence.

What is your favorite memory from Glee Club?
My favorite memory of Glee Club is singing the UNI Alma Mater in a rooftop garden in Madrid, Spain with several of my Brothers in Song.

What is your favorite food?
My comfort food is slightly overcooked spaghetti with shredded cheese and parmesan!
