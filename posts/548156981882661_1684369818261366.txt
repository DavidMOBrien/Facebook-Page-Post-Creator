Please join us this upcoming Sunday, the 22nd, as we remember the life and achievements of a very special man, Dr. Les Hale. The University of Northern Iowa Varsity Men's Glee Club will sing and memories will be shared by alumni from Northern University High School, UNI Men's Glee Club and the Les Hale Chorale.

Light refreshments provided after the service. Memorials may be directed to the UNI Foundation Les Hale Endowment Fund.
